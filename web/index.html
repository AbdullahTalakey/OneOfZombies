One Of Zombies is a mini 2D game for play and enjoy.

I would like it to be an strategy game but it is more like a kill kill game. But it still has a minimal strategy elements. It supposse you have to find the innocents survivors of the zombie town and save them. But if you don't want to you don't need to do it, actually there is not a real end of the game, you can still playing besides there are still any innocent to save or not.

Just be your own.

Instructions

Do you really need it?

It's not complicate :). You was selected, o better say, you wantted to be selecte, you wanted to come back, this is because they put you on an helicopter.

The helicopter will make a round allow the town so you can observe the situation. When you be ready press 'G' and the helicopter will look for a good place to leave you on the flat.

At this momment you are alone, run run, find the innocent survivors and bring them to the helicopter.

You can pause the game pressing 'S'.

With the 'ESC' key you will can close the game.

There are a few number of special keys just for debuggin:

  • I will show you info about the vibrations of the floor.
  • P will show you a panel with info.
  • L will show you the life left any criature has.

AI

To any thing people call intellingecy

There is a small algorithm implemented on the **tripas** of this project. It is the intellingency of the not-players criatures, zombies and innocents...

They don't look very smart, did they? :) but I have tried to offer them a bit of conscience.

The zombies feel **atraidos** for the hero and for the innocents that run over there. They don't follow you like a **brujula** they try to find a valid road to take you.

The innocents feel **atraidos** for you and the feel **miedo** of the zombies, this is because, they use to follow you, but if there is a zombie near they try to run away.

The intelligence is not on the zombies or in the innocents... actually it is on the flat.

You, the zombies and the innocents **produc√≠s** a kind of vibration on the floor that is **propagada** and in a few distance dissapear. The vibration can turn the corners but not go throw the houses, this is because the innocents and zombies can feel you on the other corner of a house, but don't can feel you if is not a corner near.

If you press the 'I' key during the game you will can see the vibrations of the criatures.
